## 1. What you need before you start

- A computer, tablet, or phone with an internet connection.
- A modern web browser (Google Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).
- The original photograph you want to edit. For this task we start from the picture below (a forest scene **without** a Jaguar):

![Original picture without the Jaguar](original_picture_no_jaguar.jpeg)

---

## Step 1 — Create a Google account

Google Gemini uses your Google account, so you need one first.

1. Open <https://accounts.google.com/signup>.
2. Click **Create account** → choose **For my personal use**.
3. Enter your **First name** and **Last name**, then click **Next**.
4. Enter your **date of birth** and **gender**, then click **Next**.
5. Choose a Gmail address (pick one of Google's suggestions or create your own), then click **Next**.
6. Create a strong **password** and confirm it, then click **Next**.
7. (Optional) Add a recovery phone number and email — this helps you recover the account later. Click **Next**.
8. Agree to the **Privacy and Terms** by clicking **I agree**.

Your Google account is now ready.

> **Already have a Google account?** Skip to Step 2.

---

## Step 2 — Open Google Gemini

1. Open <https://gemini.google.com> in your browser.
2. You will see the Gemini welcome / interface page:

![Gemini interface](gemini_interface_screenshot.jpg)

The Gemini interface has three main areas:
- **Left sidebar** — chat history and the **New chat** button.
- **Center** — the conversation area where prompts and answers appear.
- **Bottom** — the input box where you type your request, plus buttons to **upload files/images** and **send**.

---

## Step 3 — Sign in to Gemini

1. On the Gemini page, click the **Sign in** button (top-right corner).
2. Enter the Gmail address you created in Step 1 and click **Next**.
3. Enter your password and click **Next**.
4. If prompted, review the **Welcome to Gemini** terms and click **I agree** / **Get started**.

You are now inside the Gemini workspace and ready to edit images.

---

## Step 4 — Upload the original picture

1. Click **New chat** (top-left) to start a fresh conversation.
2. In the bottom input bar, click the **image upload button** (the **"+"** / image icon next to the text field).
3. Select **Upload from computer**, then choose the original picture file (`original_picture_no_jaguar.jpeg`) and click **Open**.
4. Wait until the image thumbnail appears inside the input box — this means the upload is complete.

---

## Step 5 — Write the prompt to add the Jaguar

In the same input box (right next to the uploaded image), type a clear, descriptive instruction. A good prompt for this task is:

> **Add a realistic jaguar standing naturally in the forest, matching the lighting and shadows of the scene. Make it look like it belongs in the photo.**

Tips for a good result:
- Be **specific** about the animal and where it should appear.
- Ask for **realistic lighting and shadows** so the result blends in.
- Mention the **style** (photorealistic) if the source photo is a photo.

Then press the **Send** button (or hit **Enter**).

---

## Step 6 — Generate and review the result

1. Gemini will process the request and display the **edited image** in the conversation.
2. Review the result carefully:
   - Is the Jaguar positioned naturally?
   - Do the lighting and shadows match the original photo?
   - Does the Jaguar look realistic?
3. If the result is not satisfactory, click **Modify** / **Regenerate** (or type a follow-up like *"Make the jaguar smaller and place it on the left"*) and Gemini will create a new version.
4. Repeat until you are happy with the image.

---

## Step 7 — Download the final image

1. Hover over the generated image.
2. Click the **Download** icon (a down-arrow ⬇ in the image's top-right corner), or right-click the image and choose **Save image as…**.
3. Save the file with a clear name, e.g. `added_jaguar.jpeg`.
4. Choose a location on your computer and click **Save**.

Your edited picture is now ready to use.

### Summary

1. Create / sign in to a **Google account**.
2. Open **gemini.google.com** and **sign in**.
3. Start a **new chat** and **upload** the original picture.
4. **Type a clear prompt** describing the Jaguar you want to add.
5. **Generate**, review, and refine the result.
6. **Download** the final image.

That's it — you have edited an image using generative AI.
